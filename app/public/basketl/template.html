Our Process Delivers Best-in-Class Fences

Global Purchasing Power

American Fence Company purchases hundreds of train carloads and truckloads of our top-notch fence materials from vendors all around the globe. Our buying power guarantees us the very best pricing, and we gladly pass these savings on to our customers.

Exclusive Ornamental Product

American Ornamental is American Fence Company’s own prefabricated ornamental fence product. This is an ornamental fencing option that is cost competitive and performs just as well as the name brands.

Manufacturing Division

We have our own in-house manufacturing division which gives us more flexibility and capability than your average fence company.

 

Safety

American Fence Company has in impeccable safety program that has a multiple prong approach to safety which has led to a .73 MOD rate. We have achieved five consecutive years of reduced MOD rates. We have also created dozens of industry specific safe practices and equipment certification programs

Professional Team

American Fence Company has over 40 in-house installation crews. In the last three years, we have significantly increased our employees’ wages and added multiple benefit packages, including additional PTO. We are working hard to build a sustainable employee group of professionals.

Size Matters

American Fence Company operates 10 branches across the Midwest and multiple divisions. Our 300+ employees matter. American Fence Company has performed work or delivered finished products to every state within the continental United States.

Featured Products

Commercial Custom Ornamental

With a team of fabricators, 10,000 square feet of fabrication shop and certified Computer Aided Designers, your ornamental fence will be a masterpiece! The types and styles of ornamental fencing is almost endless; limited only by your imagination.

Commercial Vinyl Fence

When it comes to fencing in your property, vinyl fencing is going to make it through the tests of time. Vinyl fencing can endure all sorts of weather without worry of deterioration, cracking or chipping. With dozens of styles and colors to choose from, vinyl is ideal for the property owner who wants a fence tailored to their needs that will last a lifetime.

Residential Chain Link Fence

With over 18 acres of inventory, American Fence Company is the largest supplier of chain link fabric, posts and materials. We carry twelve different chain link heights, two colors and a variety of gauges, so we can create the right fence to fit your needs.
